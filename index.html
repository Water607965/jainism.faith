Core Principles of Jainism

Ahimsa (Non-Violence)

Respect all life and avoid harm in thought, word, and action.

Anekantavada (Multiple Viewpoints)

Embrace diverse perspectives to seek ultimate truth.

Aparigraha (Non-Possessiveness)

Let go of material desires for spiritual liberation.
